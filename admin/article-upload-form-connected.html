Upload Article

This form uploads the PDF and cover image into Supabase Storage, then inserts the article record into your articles table automatically.

Before you use this form

1. Replace YOUR_SUPABASE_URL and YOUR_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY inside the script.
2. Make sure your table is named articles.
3. Make sure your buckets exist: article-pdfs and article-covers.
4. Make sure your RLS policies allow your logged-in admin to upload files and insert rows.
